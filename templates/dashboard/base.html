Welcome to VES cITy!

This round comprises two parts.

  1. First, there will be an AUCTION. Base Price of Rs 10,00,000 will be given to each team which is to be used for the entire game(Auction and Board game). The participants will be shown riddles based on the VES campus. You will have to guess the answer and then start bidding for that place. The base value of each place will be different. A pdf will be added to the drive link shared previously which contains a list of all the places in the auction along with their revenues per round.
  2. Now the Monopoly BOARD GAME begins! The places you got after the auction will be in a monopoly board and will be owned by you and you will get revenue if the opponent team’s pawn lands there. For example: If you won Canteen in the auction so, all the canteen-related property on the entire board will be yours. The sequence of teams for dice rolls will be decided according to the previous rounds’ points.

NOTE:

  • There will be an AUCTION. Base Price of Rs 10,00,000 will be given to each team which is to be used for the entire game(Auction and Board game)
  • You will have to bid using the Hands Raise option and after the bid, you are supposed to lower the hand, else the place will be sold to you.
  • Auctioneer’s decision will be taken as a final decision.

REMEMBER THIS:

  1. The answers of the riddles will not be given. So in case if you guess the wrong answer, then you will be bidding on that only.
  2. You will have to be very attentive throughout the game. If any team enters your property then you will have to ask for the rent. ISTE-VESIT will not be responsible for any miscommunication.
  3. If you are buying one property, for eg. Canteen then you will get all the related tiles of that property.
  4. The Bidding value of each place will increase by 10,000.
  5. If you wish to sell your property to the bank, then you will get the mortgage value 10% less than the Base price (For example Base Price is 1,00,000 then the mortgage value will be 90,000)
  6. If you wish to redeem your property from the bank, then you will have to pay 5% extra of the Base Price.
  7. After one dice roll of each team i.e total 6 dice rolls, one power card will be given to each team.
  8. You can use that power card only once throughout the game.
  9. Each power card lasts until the pawn of the player crosses “GO” i.e. if the power card is used even if the pawn is at a tile two tiles before “GO” the card will expire after two tiles.

The game will be played for exactly 90 minutes. At the end of the game, the team with the highest amount of money, active properties (sum of all the base values) will be the winner.
